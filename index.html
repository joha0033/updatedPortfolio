Tap-a-roo

Tap-a-Roo is a tap display and inventory managment system developed for the Community Development Staff at Galvanize. Every weekday, there are kegs tapped for the community members. Figuring out what is on tap has mostly been work-of-mouth or memory. Tap-a-Roo changed that. An iPad sits by the taps and displays what is on each tap. The display can be changed from the front desk via chrome extension. The inventory can also be managed from the home site.

AngularJS, Bootstrap, Node.js, Express, PostgresQL, WebSockets.io
